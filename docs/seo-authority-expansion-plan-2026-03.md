## Decision

Yes, more externally grounded research content should be created.

Reasoning:

- The current site is now technically crawl-ready but still too thin for maximum authority.
- Enterprise buyers expect visible alignment with recognized frameworks.
- AI answer systems reward recurring, well-linked, definition-rich, evidence-based content.
- The ecosystem or OS category requires stronger explanation architecture than a standard SaaS website.

The immediate objective should not be content volume alone. It should be content density around the exact themes the platform claims to solve.
