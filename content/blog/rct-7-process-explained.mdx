# The RCT-7 Process Explained

The RCT-7 process is our systematic approach to analyzing and implementing intent-driven AI. It consists of seven carefully designed steps that guide researchers and developers through the process of understanding intent and building aligned systems.

## The Seven Steps

### 1. Context Gathering
Understand the full context of the situation, including historical data, environmental factors, and stakeholder perspectives.

### 2. Intent Elicitation
Actively work to understand the underlying intent behind requests or behaviors.

### 3. Intent Validation
Verify that you've correctly understood the intent by checking with stakeholders or testing your assumptions.

### 4. Design Alignment
Create systems and processes that are explicitly aligned with the identified intent.

### 5. Implementation
Build and deploy the intent-aligned solution.

### 6. Monitoring
Continuously monitor how the system performs and whether it maintains alignment with intent.

### 7. Adaptation
Adjust and improve the system based on monitoring data and feedback.

## Real-World Application

These steps are being applied across industries, from healthcare to finance to education. The common thread is that understanding human intent leads to better, more trustworthy AI systems.

## Conclusion

The RCT-7 process represents years of research and practical experience. It's a framework we're making freely available to the broader AI community.
